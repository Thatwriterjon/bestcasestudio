1. "How long does a case study take?" → "A Single takes 2–3 weeks from customer interview to delivery. The Trio takes 3–6 weeks. We work around your client's schedule for the interview."

3. "Can I buy one case study now and add more later?" → "Absolutely. You can start with a Single and commission a Trio later. The per-case-study price stays the same — the Trio just saves you money upfront."
4. "What makes Best Case Studio different from a freelance copywriter?" → "We handle the full production stack: customer interview, video editing, landing page copy, and social assets. Most copywriters hand you a Word doc. We hand you a ready-to-publish content package."

6. "How do case studies actually improve conversion rates?" → "A case study removes the 'will this work for us?' objection by showing a real company with a real result. It shortens the buying decision for prospects at the bottom of the funnel."
7. "What if our customer can't point to a specific measurable result?" → "That's fine. Not every great case study leads with a metric. Strong stories about reduced friction, improved confidence, or better team alignment are just as persuasive to the right buyer."
8. "Why is the Trio cheaper per case study?" → "Three interviews scheduled in the same window, one editing sprint, one delivery round. The fixed overhead is shared, so we pass the saving on."
9. "Who actually runs Best Case Studio?" → "Jon McGreevy leads every project — strategy, interviews, and copy. Alex handles all video production. Jessie keeps projects on track. There's no outsourcing or AI-generated content."
10. "How does the $500 referral bounty work?" → "Refer a B2B SaaS company to us. When they make their first payment, we send you $500 cash — no cap on how many referrals you make."
